Demand

lav simpel model, som kan gøre det

Demand Volatility 
--> Coefficient of variation ofdemand in a weekly 
(7 day) moving window (SD / mean)

Margin 
--> maximum declared output aggregated across all units 
minus the demand forecast 

Lag-1 Margin 
--> same definition as above for previous day 

Past Prices 
- P_t-1 (same period previous day)
- P_t-7 (Same period previous week)
- MP_t-1 (average price previous day)

Price Volatility 
--> Coefficient of variation of spot Prices for 
each period acroos the preceeding 7 days (same 
as Demand volatility)
